About Us

Our Joyful Philosophy

Kid's World Kalamboli in Kalamboli, Navi Mumbai is a prestigious educational institution located in Kalamboli, Navi Mumbai. Established in 2013, it operates from Monday:- 8:00 am - 6:00 pm, Tuesday:- Kid's 8:00 am - 6:00 pm, Wednesday: 8:00 am - 6:00 pm, Thursday: 8:00 am - 6:00 pm, Friday: 8:00 am - 6:00 pm, Saturday: 8:00 am - 6:00 pm, Sunday: Closed, providing an excellent education while fostering a nurturing environment for students' holistic development.
